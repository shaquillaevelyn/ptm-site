Your Toastmasters Journey Starts Here!


Public Speaking is not to be feared

It can be enjoyed and Paddington Toastmasters are here to help! We are a lively and supportive club, ready to support you as you build your confidence, public speaking and communication skills.

Whether you are a complete newbie or a seasoned public speaker, you are welcome to join us at our meetings where we can improve together.



Progress with us

We pride ourselves on creating a relaxed and informal environment where you feel comfortable to participate and most importantly, have a laugh while doing so.

On becoming a member, we offer mentorship and support as you make your way through the Toastmasters International Educational Program, Pathways.

Read more about Toasmasters + Pathways



Reap the Benefits

Help us to help you to Build Leadership Skills, Maximise your Potential, in the workplace and beyond, and Enhance Self-Awareness.

The benefits are endless!

Testimonials

Hear from our members about the power of improving public speaking through Toastmasters

Joining Toastmasters 18 months ago has had such a postive effect on me. I am a far more eloquent, competent and creative speaker; and during the Covid-19 pandemic, these skills have not gone to waste. The zoom meetings are fun and a great palate cleanser from work calls! Patiently, I wait for the pandemic to come to an end so we can go back to in person meetings where I can see all the smiling faces of my fellow members.
