We are Paddington Toastmasters.
A club like no other.


Public Speaking need not to be feared.

It can be enjoyed and Paddington Toastmasters are here to help! We are a lively and supportive club, ready to support you as you build your confidence and public speaking skills.

Whether you are a complete newbie or a seasoned public speaker, you are welcome to join us at our meetings where we can improve together.



Progress with us

We pride ourselves in creating a relaxed and informal environment where you feel most comfortable to particpate and most importantly, have a laugh while doing so.

On becoming a paid member, we offer mentorship and support as you make your way though the Toastmasters International Educational Program, Pathways



Reap the Benefits

Help us to help you to Build Leadership skills, Maximise your Potential in the workplace and beyond, Enhance Self Awareness.
The benefits are endless!
